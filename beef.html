Beef and Potatoes Stew

Classic French beef stew is the ultimate cold weather comfort food, cooked in a rich and savory sauce, this beef stew recipe is comfort food at its best.

A one-pot meal that’s hearty, flavorful and loaded with tender beef morsels, potatoes, and carrots.

This simple and healthy beef stew recipe can easily be made in an instant pot, slow cooker, in the oven, or on a stovetop

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450 grams) beef stew meat, cut into cubes
  • 4 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups (1 liter) beef broth
  • Salt,pepper and dried thyme to taste

Directions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the beef cubes to the pot and brown them on all sides. This will help enhance the flavor of the stew. Once the beef is browned, remove it from the pot and set it aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté them for a few minutes until they become fragrant and slightly softened
  3. Return the beef cubes to the pot and add the diced potatoes and sliced carrots. Pour in the beef broth and sprinkle in the dried thyme.
  4. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste. Stir all the ingredients together, making sure everything is well combined
  5. Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a lid. Simmer the stew for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender and the potatoes and carrots are cooked through.
